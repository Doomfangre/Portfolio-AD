ZCS App

ZCS Animal Enrichment App

Oct 2025 - Dec 2025

My class in C.A.R.T. was tasked with rehauling an animal tracking app that the Fresno Chaffee Zoo is using. The project could be used for other Zoos in the future. My team used Figma, a software that is used to design apps, to prototype the new app. HTML and CSS were utilized for the app to function. I learned how to use figma for the first time and gained skills in collaboration with my teammates. I had to communicate with my collaborators to brainstorm and lay out the app.

The Technology Stack

    • HTML5, Vanilla JavaScript, and Figma.
      Figma was used for all of the prototyping and coding the project.

    • Concepts Learned

    • I used figma as the website for creating the wireframing and the prototype using its built-in features. It was mostly made up of div`s.
      The website was responsive enough to become a phone screen.
      This project helped me understand what goes into creating an app for the first time.

    • Challenges & Solutions

    • The Problem: The layout of the site was the hardest to manage. The buttons, navigation bar, and the user flow were very complicated to understand.
      The Solution: Since I had classmates to help with this issue, we had an easier time coming up with solutions. We figured that all of the assets would be in a location that is easiest for the user to navigate.

    • Habits of Mind in Action

    • Persistence: When coming up with solvents to problems on the website, I had to persist through the navigation issues with my peers to figure out the solution.
      Collaboration: I had to collaborate with my peers for additional help and brainstorming for asset placement.
